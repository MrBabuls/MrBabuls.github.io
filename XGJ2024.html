Xamk Game Jam 2024 Spring

  • April 29, 2024

Project description

The game was made as part of Xamk Game Jam 2024, which was organized from 5 April to 7 April 2024. The team included Tarita Karhulahti and Riku Heino. On the first day we started to plan our game and we decided to do Lost in the morgue. Players need to find all missing body parts and bring them back to the corps. This game genre is survival horror.

Description of the task

My task in the team was to be one of the programmers and 3D-models in the game jam.

Description of methods

In the game, players control their character using the keyboard's WASD keys. The adventure begins as the player awakens in the morgue, faced with a daunting task. Players need to find a way to unlock the sealed room. Along the journey towards solving this mystery, players must meticulously gather clues from their surroundings and piece together the puzzle to uncover the truth. However, as they finally enter the room, a chilling surprise awaits them.

Above are the 3D models I made for the game.

Problems and solutions

The biggest problem was the lack of time and also a lack of communication. At times we noticed that two people were doing the same thing, even though we had assigned tasks to everyone and knew who was doing what.

Final assessment

The game turned out great even though we didn't manage to build it, but the intention is to continue developing this game in the future.
