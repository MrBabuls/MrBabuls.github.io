Finnish Game Jam 2025

  • January 27, 2025

Project description

The game was developed as part of the Finnish Game Jam 2025, held from January 24 to January 26, 2025. The team included Tarita Karhulahti and Riku Heino, we had 48 hours’ time to create a game that fit the predetermined theme which was a "Bubble”. On the first day we started to plan our game and we decided to do Bubble Boom. Players embark on an adventure to solve a variety of puzzles in a single, challenging level to make it through to the finish. Each puzzle tests your problem-solving skills as you progress towards victory.

Description of the task

In the team my role involved modeling the player character, creating animations for it and developing a cartoon shader in Unity using Shader Graph for the character.

Description of methods

In the game, the player controls the character using the WASD keys for movement and the mouse to look around or interact with the environment. Players must explore the level and solve challenges to progress toward the goal.

One of the core mechanics involves collecting balls from a vending machine. The player interacts with the vending machine to retrieve the balls, which are then used as projectiles. These balls are fired from a cannon to break through a ball wall that blocks the player’s path. Successfully breaking the wall allows the player to continue their journey toward the goal.

The game focuses on exploration and problem-solving as players overcome obstacles to reach the finish line.

Above, you can see the 3D character I modeled and some animations I created in Blender.

Problems and solutions

My biggest challenge was rigging and animating the eyelids of the 3D character. Due to time running out, I couldn't continue rigging the eyelids and had to move on to ensure I could complete all of my tasks.

Final assessment

I am very pleased with our game that we developed during the game jam event. I really like the visual look and sounds of the game. We had a lot of fun throughout the entire weekend and at the same time I learned more about rigging and animating characters in Blender more efficiently.

I do feel disappointed that I wasn't able to rig the eyelids successfully, but I plan to practice face rigging and animation more in the future.
