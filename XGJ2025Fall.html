Xamk Gam Jam 2025 Fall

  • November 3, 2025

Project description

The game was made as part of Xamk Game Jam 2025 Fall, which was organized from 31 October to 2 November 2025. The team included Tarita and Riku. First day we started to plan our game and we decided to do Born to burn. Players need to find all the candles in the cemetery and light them.

Above, you can see the game's main menu.

Description of the task

In the team my role involved modeling the player character, rigging it, animating it, creating textures for it and modifying existing assets like shaders and models.

Description of methods

In the game, the player controls the character using the WASD keys for movement and the mouse to look around or shoot.

Players explore a graveyard. The mission is to relight every candle scattered across the area, but the world is dangerous. Skeletons roam the grounds and dark rain clouds threaten to extinguish the flame.

Bushes and trees may accidentally ignite, and spreading fire can harm the candle character.

Problems and solutions

We didn’t run into any major problems this time. We didn’t design a game that was too big to finish in a weekend. Instead, we kept the project as simple as possible.

Final assessment

I’m very satisfied with what we created during the weekend. I also had time to learn Adobe Substance 3D Designer and it allowed me to create a texture that can be used in a shader to give the character a painted-style surface.
